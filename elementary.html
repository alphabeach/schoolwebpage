Programs Offered

Science and Technology

Explore the wonders of science and technology through hands-on experiments and innovative projects. This program is designed to ignite curiosity and foster a love for STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics) among young learners. Students will engage in activities that challenge their critical thinking and problem-solving skills, preparing them for future academic pursuits in scientific fields.

Arts and Music

Unleash your creativity with our Arts and Music program, where students can express themselves through various artistic mediums. This program offers a comprehensive curriculum that includes visual arts, music theory, and performance arts. Students will have opportunities to showcase their talents in school exhibitions and performances, nurturing their artistic abilities and confidence.

Sports and Physical Education

Promote a healthy lifestyle and teamwork through our Sports and Physical Education program. This program emphasizes physical fitness, sportsmanship, and the importance of staying active. Students will participate in a variety of sports, from team games to individual challenges, helping them develop physical skills, discipline, and a sense of camaraderie.
